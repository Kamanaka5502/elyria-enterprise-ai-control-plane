# Enterprise Operating Model

## Purpose

This operating model defines how an enterprise can use the Elyria Enterprise AI Control Plane across business, technology, data, security, privacy, risk, and delivery teams.

---

## Operating Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Business Owner | Defines business value, operational impact, and acceptance criteria. |
| Technical Owner | Owns architecture, integration, deployment, and support model. |
| Data Owner | Confirms data scope, classification, access, lineage, and retention. |
| Security Lead | Reviews threat model, access, monitoring, escalation, and incident path. |
| Privacy / Risk Lead | Reviews sensitive data, policy requirements, and auditability. |
| AI Platform Team | Owns model, RAG, agent, evaluation, and platform implementation patterns. |
| Executive Sponsor | Resolves priority, investment, and production movement decisions. |

---

## Governance Cadence

| Cadence | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Intake Review | Classify the AI use case and determine initial risk tier. |
| Architecture Review | Resolve data, identity, model, RAG, agent, and integration design. |
| Readiness Review | Confirm required controls and evidence before pilot or production. |
| Production Review | Decide ADMIT / HOLD / REFUSE / REVALIDATE. |
| Revalidation Review | Reassess after model, data, prompt, tool, policy, or environment change. |

---

## Decision Rights

```text
Business value belongs to the business owner.
Technical design belongs to the technical owner.
Data permission belongs to the data owner.
Security posture belongs to the security lead.
Privacy and policy risk belong to privacy/risk leadership.
Production movement requires resolved ownership across all required domains.
```

---

## Operating Principle

AI governance becomes durable only when responsibility is attached to movement.

A system should not advance because it is impressive. It should advance because ownership, authority, data scope, controls, evaluation, observability, and revalidation have been resolved.
